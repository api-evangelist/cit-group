# CIT Group (cit-group)
CIT Group was a US financial holding company providing commercial financing, equipment leasing, factoring, and middle-market banking services. CIT was acquired by First Citizens BancShares in January 2022 and has been integrated into First Citizens Bank as the Commercial Banking division.

## APIs

CIT Group does not maintain a public developer portal and never published general-purpose REST APIs. Legacy customer integrations were delivered through file-based ACH/wire feeds, the OneWest online banking platform, and partner-mediated treasury connectivity. New API-based integrations for the combined institution are now exposed through First Citizens Bank.
